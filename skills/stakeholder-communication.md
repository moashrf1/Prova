# Stakeholder Communication

## When to use this
Any time a decision, delay, or trade-off needs to be communicated across teams.

## Core ideas
- **Tailor the message to the audience** — leadership wants outcomes and risks; engineers want context and constraints; clients want impact and timing.
- **Lead with the "why"** before the "what" — people accept decisions they understand the reasoning behind.
- **Manage expectations early** — surface risks and delays before they become surprises.
- **Write updates that are scannable** — the headline first, detail below.

## Common trap
Over-communicating detail to leadership or under-communicating context to engineers. Match depth to what each audience needs to act.
